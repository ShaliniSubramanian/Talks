Designing for People with Disabilitues

  1. Visual
  2. Auditory
  3. Motor
  4. Cognitive

Visual

  1. Blind
  2. Low vision
  3. Color Blindness

Blind

  1. They use screen reader
  2. Need text description of images
  3. Should be able to use the app with keyboard
  4. Proper headings

Low Vision


  1. They use screen reader or maginifiers
  2. App can be zoomed easily

Color blind

  1. Can be red defiency (pratonipa), green defecinecy (Deuteranopia), or cannot see any color (Rod Monchromacy)
  2. Reds and greens are often indistinguishable
  3. Color should not be the only way to show information.
  4. Provide suffecient contrast . WCAG 2.0 level AA requires a contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text (14 point and bold or larger, or 18 point or larger). Tool: Color contrast tool

Designing for Old users

Old users -Challenges

  1. Everyone of us going to become old and may face all or some of these?
  2. Vision Decline - Decreasing ability to focus on near tasks,Contrast sensitivity,Pupil shrinkage
  3. Hearing loss
  4. Motor- Trembling in the hands , may operate with single fingers
  5. Cognitive - shows decline in memory
